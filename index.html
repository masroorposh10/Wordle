WORDLE

    Instructions for playing the game:

  • Guess the four-letter word by typing it in the input field.
  • You have 4 chances to guess the word.
  • The letters that are correct and in the correct position will be shown in green, and the letters that are correct but in the wrong position will be shown in yellow.
  • when you win you, refresh or press start over to play again.
  • Click Start over to try again if you lose.
  • Click Hint to check for hints.
